\section*{Question 1:}

Given a plane \(P\) with equation \(2x - 5y + 7z = 9\), and a line with the parametric form \(L: \left\{\begin{array}{rl} x(t) = & 1 + 4t \\ y(t) = & 2 - kt \\ z(t) = & 1 + 6t \end{array}\right.\), find a value of \(k\) such that \(L\) is parallel to \(P\).
